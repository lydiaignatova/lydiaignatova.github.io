Better Baking

Hello Kitty is learning to code to make it easier for her to bake! She sets up this program in Python:

apple = 200
cinnamon = 145
nutmeg = 80
butter = 120
flour = 600 
flour = flour + cinnamon 
cinnamon = nutmeg + butter
butter = apple // nutmeg 
fish = apple
apple = butter
butter = fish 
cinnamon = flour - 2 * apple 
apple = nutmeg * 3 % 200

After all of this happens, what will apple + cinnamon be?
