Clue 7

Scattered Recipes

Hello Kitty was organizing her recipes when an earthquake caused the pages to scatter. She had labeled each recipe with an ID number. She remembers that any recipe with an ID number divisible by 3 is gluten-free. Here are the recipe ID numbers she found: 6603501 0684228 6470393 6858272 3307329 8599839 4714194 0741720 6128001 7282925 5829958 8034135. What percentage of these recipes are gluten-free?
