Word Permutations

Hello Kitty wants to visit her friends a couple of times! Trace through this code and count up how many times the letter “o” is printed out by python! 
friends = [“My Melody”, “Kuromi”, “Cinnamoroll”, “Keroppi”, and “Pompompurin”]
for i in range(2):
    for name in friends:
        print(“Hello”, name”)
print(“So long, amigos!”)
