Walk Probability

Hello Kitty loves going on walks, but only under certain weather conditions. She checks the forecast each day for sunshine (S) and wind (W). She will go for a walk if it is sunny or not windy. The chance of sunshine on a given day is 70%. The chance of it being windy on a given day is 40%. What is the probability that Hello Kitty will go for a walk on a randomly chosen day?
